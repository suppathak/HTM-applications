HTM Applications¶

The Hierarchical Temporal Memory (HTM) algorithm is based on the well understood principles and core building blocks of the Thousand Brains Theory, which is the core-model based, sensory-motor framework of intelligence putting together the neuroscience research developed at Numenta founded by Jeff hawkings. It aims to reflect the functioning of the human cortex. It focusses on three main properties: sequence learning, continual learning, and sparse distributed representations.

The project aims to explore the applications of HTM primarily focussing on Anomaly Detection technique.

The related upstream repo for the projects are:

  • Numenta Anomaly Benchmark

  • Nupic
